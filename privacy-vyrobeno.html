Privacy Policy – Vyrobeno (CZ + EN)

English

Effective date: April 3, 2026

1. Overview

Vyrobeno is an iOS app by Mast Dev Studio for scanning EAN/UPC barcodes and showing the code registration country.

2. Data We Process

The app does not collect or transmit personal user data.

3. Camera

Camera access is used only to scan barcodes on the user’s device.

4. Internet and Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party advertising or analytics tracking services.
